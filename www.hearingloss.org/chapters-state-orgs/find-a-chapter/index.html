Find a Chapter

To see if there is an HLAA Chapter, State Organization, State Chapter Coordinator, or Support Group in your area, find your state on the map and click on the pin for more details or scroll to the bottom for a listing. How to search: enter the city or state into the “Enter Address” field and the listing will filter accordingly. If there aren’t any chapters in your area, contact the HLAA Chapter Coordinator about starting a Chapter today!

Virtual Meetings: Many HLAA Chapters are holding virtual meetings on line via Zoom, especially during the pandemic. Please reach out to individual chapter leaders for meeting options. Chapters welcome anyone to meetings. If you need information or want to meet others with hearing loss, please reach out to our chapters.
