Our trainers can assist others in the successful integration of hearing assistive technology to support individual hearing and communication needs at home, work, school, and the community.

If you are interested in hosting an N-CHATT community training session, check the map for a trainer in your area. To make a request for a training session, click on theÂ SCHEDULE A TRAININGÂ button and complete the N-CHATT Scheduling form.
